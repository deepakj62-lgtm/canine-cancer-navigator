# Canine Cancer Navigator

**Free, open-source cancer treatment navigator for dogs.**

When your dog is diagnosed with cancer, the information gap is brutal. Most owners get a standard chemo protocol and never hear about immunotherapy, precision medicine, or clinical trials that could change the outcome. This tool exists to close that gap.

## What It Does

Enter your dog's cancer details — type, stage, breed, biomarkers — and get a personalized, multi-layer treatment pipeline:

- **Layer 0: Standard of Care** — Evidence-based frontline protocols (CHOP, carboplatin, etc.)
- **Layer 1: Immunotherapy Channels** — Cancer vaccines, checkpoint inhibitors, adoptive cell therapy
- **Layer 2: Precision Medicine** — Genomic profiling, targeted therapies (FidoCure, ImpriMed)
- **Layer 3: TME Modulation** — Tumor microenvironment strategies
- **Layer 4: Monitoring** — Liquid biopsy, imaging schedules, response tracking

- Soft Tissue Sarcoma

## Missing Data Suggestions

Don't have all the test results? The tool still works. It generates the best pipeline it can with what you have, then tells you:
- What additional tests would improve your results
- Why each test matters for your dog's specific cancer
- How to get each test done
- Approximate costs

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any browser — no installation, no server, no account needed
2. Select your dog's cancer type and stage
3. Add any biomarkers, mutations, or treatment history you have
4. Click "Generate Treatment Pipeline"
5. Review the multi-layer treatment plan with your veterinarian

## Why This Exists

This project was inspired by a dog owner who used AI tools to design a cancer treatment for his dog when vets said there was nothing left to try. Most pet owners don't have the technical background to do that kind of research. This tool gives every dog owner access to the same kind of treatment mapping — standard care, immunotherapy, precision medicine, clinical trials — all cross-referenced against published veterinary oncology data.

## Philosophy

- **Free forever.** No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads.
- **Open source.** MIT license. Fork it, improve it, share it.
- **No data collection.** Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Not a replacement for your vet.** This is an information tool to help you have better conversations with your veterinary oncologist.
